Welcome to TE ATATU SQUASH CLUB

Te Atatu Squash Club is located at Club Physical Te Atatu. We have 6 courts and are home to 45 members, ranging from 7 to 87 years old.

TASC is ranked 4th stongest in the country by NZ Squash, and are the newly crowned New Zealand Club Squash champions.
