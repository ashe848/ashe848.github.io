ULearn Physics is an initiative founded in 2015 by the ULearn Foundation. We provide quality educational material to New Zealand schools and students for free.

This website, ULearn Physics, is specifically for NCEA Level 3 Physics students, and is due to be completed at the end of 2015.
Check out our material on Internals, Mechanics, Waves, and Electricity!

Please note that our site is designed with the idea of revision and consolidation of knowledge in mind, so it's not intended to be a replacement for teaching nor do we guarantee to cover every possible explanation in great detail. Our suggestion to getting the best grades in L3 Physics is to listen in class and look over the material on ULearn Physics to make sure you understand everything!
We are open to comments on this, but we believe that our ULearn Physics site is most effective as a substitute for taking notes, provided you can remember the full story when exams come!

If you would like one-on-one tutoring from our top physics scholars at Rutherford College, or to book a private group session, drop us a line at physics@ULearn.co.nz.
